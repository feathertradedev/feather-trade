<Steps>
1. Open the exact pool and choose **Create Position** or **Add More**.
2. Enter token amounts and choose a range of no more than 69 bins.
3. Select Spot, Curve, or Bid-Ask and inspect every projected weight.
4. Review minimum amounts, slippage, bin tolerance, deadline, recipient, refund destination, and gas estimate.
5. Complete each required approval.
6. Review refreshed balances and pool state, then submit the liquidity transaction.
</Steps>

Adding to bins you already own increases the same fungible balances. The application may preserve deposit history for accounting, but it must not imply that overlapping deposits remain separately withdrawable.

<Warning title="Amounts can require more than one transaction">
Each nonzero ERC-20 side can need its own approval before the final add. Approval success is not position creation.
</Warning>
