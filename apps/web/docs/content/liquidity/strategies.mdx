Strategies calculate relative weights across the selected bins. They do not create durable strategy accounts and do not rebalance automatically.

| Strategy | Distribution | Typical tradeoff |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Spot | Equal weight across eligible bins | Simple, even coverage |
| Curve | More weight toward the active price | Greater near-market concentration |
| Bid-Ask | More weight toward range edges | More edge concentration and different conversion behavior |

Each token side is normalized independently. The preview must match the exact bin IDs and weights sent for review.

<Risk title="A strategy is an input, not a forecast">
No distribution guarantees fee income, time in range, protection from conversion, or better performance than holding the tokens.
</Risk>
