A liquidity range is a contiguous selection of price bins. Narrow ranges concentrate capital near selected prices. Wide ranges spread it across more prices.

## Position relative to the market

- Bins above the active bin accept token X.
- Bins below the active bin accept token Y.
- The active bin can accept both assets.
- A range wholly on one side can support a direct one-sided deposit.

Range handles, price fields, bin IDs, and displayed composition must stay synchronized. Feather supports at most 69 bins in one prepared range and rejects larger selections.

<Warning title="The active bin can move during review">
A changed active bin can alter eligible composition, fees, or transaction parameters. Feather refreshes and requires a new review instead of submitting stale arrays.
</Warning>
