## Partial withdrawal

Choose selected bins and an exact percentage. The review calculates a burn amount per live balance and protects minimum token X and token Y outputs. A successful partial withdrawal leaves remaining balances in the position.

## Full exit

Full Exit means 100% of every live positive bin balance owned by the connected wallet in the selected pool. A subset or percentage below 100% is never labeled a full exit.

<Steps>
1. Refresh the complete owned-bin set.
2. Reconcile live balances and pool reserves at one current state.
3. Review operator approval scope when required.
4. Inspect expected and minimum outputs, gas, and output asset policy.
5. Simulate and submit the exact burn transaction.
6. Confirm received assets and remaining bin balances.
</Steps>

<Risk title="Withdrawal realizes the current composition">
A position may return mostly or entirely one asset after market movement. A protected zero on one side can be valid when the other side remains protected.
</Risk>
