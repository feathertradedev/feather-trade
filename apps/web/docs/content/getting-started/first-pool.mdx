Open a pool from Discover and verify its exact identity before trading or providing liquidity.

## Identity

Confirm the token names and addresses, pool contract address, token order, and bin step. Feather uses the chain selected by the deployed application; verify the requested chain in your wallet before signing. Two pools can use the same tokens while having different addresses and economic behavior.

## Market state

Review the displayed price, liquidity distribution, fee parameters, and recent price activity. Missing USD values are not the same as zero value. Empty pools have no executable swap liquidity.

## Choose an action

- **Swap** exchanges one pool asset for the other.
- **Create Position** distributes supplied assets across selected bins.
- **Manage** inspects or removes liquidity already owned by the connected wallet.

<Callout title="Pool context stays attached">
Action panels retain the selected pool. If the address or bin step changes, Feather requires a new review rather than silently substituting another pool.
</Callout>
