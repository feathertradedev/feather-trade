## Connect

Choose **Connect**, select the intended wallet if more than one is available, and approve account access in the wallet. Feather displays the connected address after the provider confirms access.

Common connection failures include a locked wallet, rejected permission, no installed provider, or a provider that has not authorized the site. Unlock the wallet or grant access, then retry from the application.

## Switch networks

When prompted, inspect the requested chain details in the wallet. Reject an unexpected chain. After a successful switch, Feather reloads chain-specific pools, balances, approvals, and positions.

## Disconnect or change accounts

Disconnecting clears the local connection. Changing accounts invalidates any unsubmitted review because owner, balance, allowance, and recipient context changed.

<Warning title="A submitted wallet request cannot be cancelled by editing the page">
After the wallet receives or broadcasts a transaction, later form edits create a separate draft. They do not alter the original request or hash.
</Warning>
