Every Feather action is an onchain transaction controlled by your wallet. Review the complete transaction request and understand the risks before signing.

## Market and liquidity risk

Prices can move between review and confirmation. Swaps may receive less than the expected amount, subject to the selected minimum. Liquidity positions can become one-sided, move out of range, or lose value relative to holding the tokens separately.

## Contract and token risk

Contracts can contain defects. Tokens can have unusual transfer behavior, mutable controls, blacklists, or misleading symbols. Feather restricts unsupported assets, but an allowlisted asset is not a promise of safety or value.

## Approval risk

An ERC-20 allowance lets a spender transfer an approved token amount. A Liquidity Book operator approval can cover every bin balance issued by one pair contract. Verify both addresses and scopes.

<Risk title="Never sign under pressure">
Reject any request with an unexpected account, chain, token, spender, recipient, value, or calldata target. A legitimate interface will let you stop and review again.
</Risk>
