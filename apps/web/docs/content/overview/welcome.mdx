Feather is a non-custodial exchange built around Liquidity Book pools. You trade from your own wallet, or provide liquidity across selected price bins to earn a share of eligible trading fees.

## Start with a goal

- **Trade:** discover a pool, review its exact address and bin step, then swap inside that pool.
- **Provide liquidity:** choose a range and distribution strategy, approve the required assets, and create a position.
- **Manage a position:** inspect current token claims, add more liquidity, withdraw part of the position, or exit every live bin.
- **Build directly:** review the public contract architecture and integration safety requirements.

<Risk title="Your funds remain your responsibility">
Feather cannot recover wallet keys, reverse confirmed transactions, guarantee returns, or prevent losses caused by market movement or contract risk.
</Risk>

## What you need

Use a supported EVM wallet, select Robinhood Chain, keep enough ETH for gas, and verify every token and contract address before approving a transaction.
