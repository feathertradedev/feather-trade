Feather is designed around exact pool context. The pool address, token order, bin step, current price, fees, liquidity, and transaction settings remain visible while you trade or manage liquidity.

## For traders

- Choose the exact pool used for execution.
- Review expected output, minimum received, fees, price impact, deadline, and current quote freshness.
- Simulate the exact transaction before it reaches the wallet.

## For liquidity providers

- Place capital into a narrow or wide range of price bins.
- Choose Spot, Curve, or Bid-Ask distributions.
- Provide one asset when the selected range supports direct one-sided liquidity.
- Withdraw selected bins or exit the complete pool position.

<Callout title="Control is not protection from loss">
More precise controls let you express a strategy. They do not remove impermanent loss, out-of-range risk, token risk, gas cost, or smart-contract risk.
</Callout>
