## Before encoding

- Confirm Robinhood Chain mainnet and the current verified deployment.
- Attest factory, pair code, token order, bin step, and hooks.
- Reject unsupported tokens, presets, versions, and paths.
- Distinguish native ETH from WETH.

## Before submission

- Bind sender, recipient, refund destination, target, calldata, and native value.
- Use explicit minimum outputs and bounded deadlines.
- Disclose exact ERC-20 allowances and pair-wide operator approvals.
- Refresh balance, allowance, active state, and quote after every prerequisite.
- Simulate the exact transaction from the final sender.

## After submission

- Track the hash, replacements, receipt status, and required confirmations.
- Reconcile token balances, native balance, live bin balances, and expected events.
- Treat unknown submission, reorganization, revert, and delayed views as distinct states.

<Risk title="Fail closed">
If identity, completeness, freshness, or simulation cannot be established, do not construct or submit the write.
</Risk>
