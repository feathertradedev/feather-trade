## Review to submission

Before wallet handoff, editing a bound field cancels the unsubmitted review. After handoff, the original request remains independent from later edits.

## Submitted

Once a transaction hash exists, Feather tracks it under the submitting account, chain, action, and contract context. Reloading the page should not replay it.

## Receipt and confirmations

A successful receipt proves execution in one block. The application can require additional confirmations before treating it as canonical success. Other views may take longer to reflect the result.

## Replacement or reorganization

A wallet can replace a pending transaction. A chain reorganization can temporarily remove a prior receipt. Feather keeps related hashes linked and reconciles whether the action is re-included, absent, reverted, or confirmed again.

<Callout title="Pending is not success">
Only the required confirmed receipt state establishes completion. A wallet prompt or broadcast alone does not.
</Callout>
