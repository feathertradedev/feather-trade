The pool workspace keeps market context visible while you inspect or prepare an action.

## Persistent identity

The header shows token order, full pool address, network, bin step, fee information, current price, inverse price, and freshness. Copy the full address when comparing it with an explorer or wallet request.

## Workspace regions

- The market view shows price history and liquidity distribution.
- The position view shows holdings owned by the connected wallet in this pool.
- History distinguishes swaps, liquidity additions, and removals.
- The action rail switches between Create Position, Swap, and Manage without changing pools.

On mobile, these regions become tabs, drawers, or sheets. The selected pool and transaction review remain available without horizontal page scrolling.
