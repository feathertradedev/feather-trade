A confirmed pool can exist before it has liquidity. The workspace displays its initial price and creation state without inventing volume, value, or reserves.

## What is unavailable

Swaps remain disabled until compatible liquidity exists. Missing activity is not an error, and absent USD metrics must not be shown as zero-value trading.

## Add initial liquidity

Choose **Create Position**, then select a range, distribution, and token amounts. The creation transaction and liquidity transaction are separate unless the review explicitly proves otherwise.

If pool creation succeeds but the liquidity step is rejected or reverts, the pool still exists. Reopen it, refresh the active price and balances, and prepare a new liquidity review. Do not retry pool creation.

<Warning title="Creation does not guarantee a market">
A new contract address has no inherent liquidity, users, price accuracy, or asset quality.
</Warning>
